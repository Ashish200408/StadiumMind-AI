# StadiumMind AI - Demo Script

**Target Duration**: 5-7 Minutes
**Target Audience**: Google Prompt Wars Judges

## Pre-Demo Setup
1. Ensure `.env.local` is configured with a valid `VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY`.
2. Run `npm run build` and `npm run preview` to simulate a production-like environment.
3. Open the browser to `http://localhost:4173/dashboard`.
4. Open the Developer Console (F12) to occasionally show Telemetry logs (optional).

## Phase 1: Introduction (1 Min)
- **Action**: Land on the `ExecutiveDashboard`.
- **Talk Track**: 
  > "Welcome to StadiumMind AI, a next-generation unified intelligence platform designed for complex venue operations. Today, we're not just looking at a dashboard; we're looking at a system that fuses deterministic physics simulations, domain-specific intelligence engines, and the Google Gemini Operations Copilot into a single cohesive brain."
- **Highlight**: Point out the Overall Stadium Health (e.g., 90%) and the Operational Comparison Panel showing real-time changes against the previous snapshot.

## Phase 2: Injecting Chaos - The Simulation (1.5 Mins)
- **Action**: In the Demo Scenario Selector, click "High Crowd Risk".
- **Talk Track**: 
  > "Let's see how the system handles a crisis. I'm injecting a deterministic 'Crowd Crush Risk' at the North Gate. Notice how our core engines detect this instantaneously."
- **Highlight**: 
  - Show the Overall Readiness Score dip.
  - Show the Executive Notifications feed light up with a Critical Alert.
  - Show the Action Priority List update instantly with "Deploy Crowd Control at North Gate".

## Phase 3: AI Explainability & Decision Support (1.5 Mins)
- **Action**: Click on the new recommendation in the Decision Support Panel.
- **Talk Track**: 
  > "StadiumMind doesn't just give orders; it explains them. By clicking this recommendation, Gemini analyzes the current deterministic snapshot—the crowd density, the threshold limits—and generates a human-readable explanation."
- **Highlight**: Show the Explainability Panel. Point out the Confidence Score and the exact data points Gemini used to derive the reasoning. Emphasize: *No hallucinations, just pure data-driven inference.*

## Phase 4: Automated Reporting (1 Min)
- **Action**: Go to the Report Generator panel. Select "Incident After-Action" and click Generate.
- **Talk Track**: 
  > "Once the situation is handled, executives need reports. Instead of spending hours compiling data, Gemini instantly drafts a comprehensive Markdown report summarizing the incident, the response, and operational highlights."
- **Highlight**: Open the generated report in the Report History Panel. Show the Export Controls (Markdown / PDF).

## Phase 5: Conclusion & Future Vision (1 Min)
- **Action**: Navigate to `/health` to show the Health Dashboard.
- **Talk Track**: 
  > "StadiumMind AI proves that when we combine strict deterministic logic with the analytical power of Gemini, we get a system that is both incredibly robust and highly adaptable. This architecture is production-ready and scales easily to any large venue. Thank you."
